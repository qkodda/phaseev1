Help Center

Getting Started

How do I sign up or log in?

You can create an account using your email address. After signing up, you'll receive a verification email. Once verified, you can log in and start using Phazee. If you already have an account, simply enter your email and password to sign in.

What is onboarding and why is it required?

When you first sign up, you'll complete a brief onboarding process to set up your profile. This includes selecting your content niche, target audience, preferred platforms, and content style. This information helps our AI generate more relevant and personalized content ideas for you.

Using Phazee

How do I generate content ideas?

On the homepage, tap the Generate button to create 7 new AI-powered content ideas based on your profile. You can also tap the Bolt icon to generate random ideas without using your profile preferences.

How does swiping work?

Swipe right or tap the heart button to save an idea to your Drawing Board for later. Swipe left or tap the X button to pass on an idea and move to the next one.

Can I edit my saved ideas?

Yes! Tap any saved idea to expand it, then tap the edit button to customize the title, hook, content details, and more. Save your changes before scheduling or closing.

How do I schedule content?

From an expanded idea card, tap the schedule button to add it to your content calendar. Select a date and the idea will be scheduled for that day. View all your scheduled content in the Calendar tab.

How do I update my profile preferences?

Go to your Profile page by tapping the profile icon in the top header. From there, you can update your niche, audience, platforms, and content style at any time.

Subscription & Billing

How does the free trial work?

Phazee offers a 3-day free trial. After the trial ends, your subscription automatically renews at $4.99 per month unless canceled. You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID subscription settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID settings: Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Phazee > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Note: Deleting the app does not cancel your subscription.

Features & Updates

Why are notification settings disabled?

Push notifications are coming soon! We're working on features like Daily Ideas reminders, Idea Counter alerts, and Holiday content reminders. The notification toggles are currently disabled but will be enabled in a future update.

Troubleshooting

I can't log in to my account

Make sure you're using the correct email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen to receive a reset link via email. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

How is my data protected?

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We do not sell your personal information. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy in Settings.
