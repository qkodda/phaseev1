Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate content ideas?

On the homepage, tap the refresh icon button to generate 7 new AI-powered content ideas based on your profile.

How does the swipe feature work?

Swipe left or tap the X button to skip an idea. Swipe right or tap the heart button to pin an idea for later.

Can I edit pinned ideas?

Yes! Tap any pinned idea to view and edit its details before scheduling.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from Settings > Manage Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

How do I update my content preferences?

Go to your Profile page to update your content type, platform, audience, and other preferences.
